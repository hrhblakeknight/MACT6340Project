Featured Project

INCONGRUOUS ELEMENTS

Incongruous Elements is a captivating exploration of nature’s fragments through the lens of 3D animation. By merging various natural objects and simulating dynamic effects such as smoke, fabric movement, and fire, this project emphasizes the unexpected beauty in their coexistence. Each piece is a testament to the skillful integration of these elements, transforming them into a cohesive and mesmerizing visual experience. It is the hopes of the artist that he will market these unique NFT pieces in tandem with Muse frame technologies.

About the Artist

Blake Knight is a multifaceted creative professional based in Los Angeles. With a rich background in multimedia design, creative writing, and communications, Blake has honed his skills across various creative industries, using words to crystallize emotions via creative experiences. Outside of professional endeavors, Blake is an avid gamer, artist, and gardener, and enjoys hosting dinner parties and traveling to destinations like Italy and Iceland. Currently pursuing a master's degree in creative technology, Blake is exploring the frontiers of AI, digital design, AR, and VR, preparing to shape the future of interactive media.
